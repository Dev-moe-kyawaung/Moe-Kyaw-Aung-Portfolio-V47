Frequently Asked Questions

What is your development approach?

I follow a clean architecture approach combined with MVVM pattern. This ensures code maintainability, testability, and scalability. Every project starts with thorough requirements analysis and technical design before implementation.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! All projects include support periods ranging from 2 weeks (Basic) to 3 months (Enterprise). I also offer extended support packages and can be retained for ongoing maintenance and feature development.

How do you handle security in mobile apps?

Security is paramount. I implement encryption for sensitive data, use secure preferences for storage, implement biometric authentication, and follow OWASP Mobile Top 10 guidelines. Regular security audits are part of the development process.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timeline depends on project complexity. Simple apps: 2-4 weeks. Standard enterprise apps: 6-12 weeks. Complex systems: 3-6 months. I'm flexible and can discuss custom arrangements based on your specific requirements.

Do you work with existing codebases?

Absolutely. I can refactor legacy code, optimize performance, implement new features in existing apps, and help teams migrate to modern architectures. I provide thorough code reviews and documentation.

What are your availability and communication practices?

I offer flexible engagement models including full-time, part-time, and project-based arrangements. Communication happens via email, video calls, and Slack. I provide regular status updates and maintain transparency throughout the project lifecycle.
